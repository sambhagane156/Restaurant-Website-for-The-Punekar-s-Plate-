Welcome

At The Punekar’s Plate, we bring you an authentic culinary experience that celebrates the flavors of Maharashtra and beyond. Located in the heart of Pune, our restaurant serves traditional Maharashtrian delicacies along with a variety of multi-cuisine dishes — all crafted to satisfy every Punekar’s palate.

Misal Pav – The Pride of Pune

A spicy, flavorful Maharashtrian curry made with sprouted moth beans, topped with farsan, onions, and a squeeze of lemon — best enjoyed with freshly toasted pav. This dish perfectly represents the fiery and vibrant taste of Pune!

Learn More

2. Puran Poli – Sweet Soul of Maharashtra

A festive favorite, Puran Poli is a soft, sweet flatbread stuffed with a fragrant mix of jaggery and chana dal — a must-have during Holi, Gudi Padwa, or Diwali.

Learn More

Thalipeeth – The Nutritious Delight

A multigrain flatbread packed with flavor and nutrition — Thalipeeth is a traditional Maharashtrian breakfast or dinner dish.
